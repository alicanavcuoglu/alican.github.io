The introduction
Patikada is the local freelancing network of Azerbaijan, provides a solution to the challenge of finding reliable and skilled workers for businesses and individuals. By connecting freelancers with potential clients, Patikada makes it easier for businesses to access the talent they need on a project-by-project basis. This can be especially beneficial for small businesses or startups that may not have the resources to hire full-time employees.
Homepage
The challenge
Patikada provides freelancers with a convenient platform to find new clients and grow their businesses. With its comprehensive job listings, bidding systems, and communication tools, Patikada is a valuable resource for both freelancers and businesses.
‍
One of the main challenges we faced was creating a platform that was easy to use and navigate, while also providing all the necessary features and tools for both freelancers and businesses. We approached this challenge by conducting user research and testing different design and functionality options.
To address the needs of both freelancers and businesses, we implemented a range of features on the platform, including a job search and application system for freelancers, and a profile and portfolio system for showcasing their skills and experience. We also included tools for businesses to post job listings, review freelancers' portfolios, and connect with them through the platform.
